About

Hi, I’m Steven Itti Leon; someone fascinated by the Deep Learning space, particularly the reasoning behind model behavior, math behind it, and its use cases. My interest lies at the intersection of ML theory, modern deep learning architectures, and real-world systems.

I enjoy breaking down complex ideas and papers, experimenting with models, and building ML solutions. I’m driven by curiosity and a belief that good AI starts with strong fundamentals and not just more parameters. It also helps that this space is ever-evolving so my curiosity is quenched.

This "blog" but more so an archive; is where I document that journey: lessons learned, concepts demystified, experiments that worked, and a few that definitely didn’t (those are usually more educational I swear).

If you’re a fellow learner scrolling through the About section looking to know more about the author out of curiosity - welcome and stick around; I promise the gradients won’t vanish every time :)
